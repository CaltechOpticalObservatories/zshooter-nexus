# Science

```{container} zs-lead

Astro2020 identified time-domain and multi-messenger astrophysics as a priority for the coming decade. Rubin, Roman, Euclid, gravitational-wave facilities, and high-energy missions will provide rapidly evolving and often faint targets. Gaia, Rubin, and LISA will provide compact binaries that require both precision spectroscopy and fast photometry.


ZShooter will support time-domain and multi-messenger astrophysics with
- Simultaneous 0.31 to 2.45 um spectroscopy.
- Resolving powers from ≈ 1,000 (after binning) to ≈ 40,000 (with narow slits or AO enhancement).
- Three-band qCMOS imaging.
- Rapid target-of-opportunity response.

```

:::{grid-item-card} Kilonovae

Low-resolution optical and NIR spectral sequences of neutron-star merger counterparts.

:::{grid-item-card} Young supernovae
:link: science/young-supernovae
:link-type: doc

Flash spectroscopy and velocity-resolved measurements of circumstellar material during the first hours and days.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Relativistic explosions
:link: science/relativistic-explosions
:link-type: doc

Rapid low-resolution spectroscopy and three-band imaging of GRB afterglows and luminous fast blue optical transients.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Tidal disruption events
:link: science/tidal-disruption-events
:link-type: doc

Optical and NIR spectroscopy of nuclear transients, including host-contaminated events.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} High-redshift supernovae
:link: science/high-z-supernovae
:link-type: doc

Optical and NIR classification and host redshifts for supernovae at high redshift.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Nebular supernovae
:link: science/nebular-supernovae
:link-type: doc

Late-time line luminosities and profiles that constrain ejecta composition, progenitor mass, and power source.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Binaries
:link: science/binaries
:link-type: doc

Radial velocities and chemical abundances of compact-object binaries, plus phase-resolved spectroscopy of ultracompact systems.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} High-speed qCMOS imaging
:link: science/high-speed-imaging
:link-type: doc

qCMOS photometry of ultracompact binaries, optical pulsars, and accreting compact objects.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Primordial D/H
:link: science/primordial-dh
:link-type: doc

High-S/N spectroscopy of deuterium absorption in z = 2 to 3 QSO sightlines.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} White dwarf systems
:link: science/white-dwarf-systems
:link-type: doc

Atmospheric abundances, gaseous debris disks, and NIR dust emission.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Gravitational lensing
:link: science/lensing
:link-type: doc

Spectroscopy of lensed galaxies, quasars, and transients over a range of resolving powers.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Protostellar accretion
:link: science/protostellar-accretion
:link-type: doc

Simultaneous Balmer-continuum, hydrogen-line, and outflow diagnostics.
:::

:::{grid-item-card} Solar-system objects
:link: science/solar-system
:link-type: doc

Reflectance, gas-production, and isotope measurements for comets and small bodies.
:::
