Tidal Disruption Events
=======================

.. container:: zs-lead

   TDEs are a broad-coverage nuclear-transient case with occasional genuine prompt-response pressure. They also force the site to tell a cleaner story about host subtraction, spectrophotometric anchoring, and why the imager exists.

.. container:: zs-placeholder zs-internal-only

   Suggested seed figure: one optical+UV TDE spectroscopy panel or dust-echo evolution figure.

Why ZShooter
------------

- Optical+NIR leverage helps from early blue continua through later dust-echo behavior.
- Nuclear contrast and repeatability matter.
- Some events are scheduled follow-up; a smaller subset really want rapid response.

Current design pressure
-----------------------

This case keeps pressure on broad coverage, usable spectrophotometry, and the acquisition / context-imaging story.
