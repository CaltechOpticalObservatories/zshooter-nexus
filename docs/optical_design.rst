.. container:: zs-note

   **Key deltas from conceptual review**

   - Two spectrometers, not three
   - Three channels per spectrometer
   - 2k x 2k active detector areas, six total detectors not nine
   - Reduced anamorphism from ±30% to ±10%
   - No grating or detector mosaics, not five
   - Slit viewing cameras
