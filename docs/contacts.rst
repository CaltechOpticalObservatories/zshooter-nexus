Contacts
========

Leadership
----------

.. grid:: 1 1 2 2
   :gutter: 2

   .. grid-item-card:: Shrinivas (Shri) R. Kulkarni
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Principal Investigator**

      ``srk@astro.caltech.edu``

      Shri Kulkarni is Caltech’s George Ellery Hale Professor of Astronomy and Planetary Science. He has played a
      central role in modern time-domain astronomy through leadership in major transient surveys including the
      Palomar Transient Factory and the Zwicky Transient Facility.

   .. grid-item-card:: Mansi M. Kasliwal
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Co-Principal Investigator**

      ``mansi@astro.caltech.edu``

      Mansi Kasliwal is Professor of Astronomy at Caltech and Director of Palomar Observatory. Her research focuses
      on supernovae, kilonovae, neutron-star mergers, and other fast transients, and she is a leading figure in
      survey-driven time-domain astronomy.

   .. grid-item-card:: Jeb Bailey
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Instrument Architect**

      ``jibailey@caltech.edu``

      Jeb Bailey is a astronomer and staff engineer at Caltech with two decades of experience in astronomical
      instrumentation spanning spectrographs for Magellan and Keck, superconducting photon-counting cameras, autonomous
      observatories, fiber systems, embedded electronics, and data-reduction pipelines.

   .. grid-item-card:: Christoffer Fremling
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Science Team Lead**

      ``fremling@caltech.edu``

      Christoffer Fremling is a staff astronomer at Caltech whose work focuses on supernovae and time-domain
      astronomy. He leads the ZTF Bright Transient Survey and developed the SNIascore machine-learning system
      for automated supernova classification.

Project Management
------------------

.. grid:: 1 1 2 2
   :gutter: 2

   .. grid-item-card:: Richard Dekany
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Project Manager**

      Richard Dekany is the assistant director of Caltech's optical and infrared instrumentation group and an
      adaptive-optics leader with a long record of major AO system design and observatory instrument development.
      His work spans Keck and thirty-meter-class AO architecture, Palomar AO development, and multiple current
      instrumentation efforts.

   .. grid-item-card:: Jocelyn Ferrara
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Systems Engineer**

      Jocelyn Ferrara is ZShooter’s Systems Engineer. She has significant prior science-operations and instrumentation
      experience, including from her time at Gemini with efforts in operations and system integration. She is also
      systems engineer for the LRIS2 and HISPEC instruments.

   .. grid-item-card:: Marc Kassis
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **WKMO Instrument Program Manager**

      Marc Kassis is Instrument Program Manager at the W. M. Keck Observatory. He has long been a key figure in Keck
      instrument development, observatory interfaces, and program-level coordination across the Keck instrument suite.


Subsystem Leads
---------------

.. grid:: 1 1 2 2
   :gutter: 2

   .. grid-item-card:: Jason Fucik
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Optical Lead**

      Jason Fucik is an optical instrumentation engineer at Caltech with extensive experience in spectrograph and
      optical-system design. He has held major roles in WFOS, LRIS-2, and other large observatory instrumentation efforts.

   .. grid-item-card:: Dan J. Reiley
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **ZImager Optical Engineer**

      Dan Reiley is an Instrument Engineer at Caltech. He has contributed to NGPS and
      ZShooter optical development as part of Caltech’s current spectrograph instrumentation program.

   .. grid-item-card:: Bob Weber
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Mechanical Lead**

      Bob Weber is part of the Caltech instrumentation team and has contributed to major optical-instrument
      development efforts including LRIS-2, KWFI, and ZShooter. His work has centered on the mechanical
      realization of advanced observatory instruments.

   .. grid-item-card:: Prakriti Gupta
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Software & Controls Lead**

      Prakriti Gupta works on instrument software, integration, and detector-facing systems within the Caltech
      observatory instrumentation program. She has contributed to NGPS, HISPEC, and ZShooter development.

   .. grid-item-card:: Yashvi Sharma
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Simulator & DRP Lead**

      Yashvi Sharma is a Caltech astronomer whose work combines supernova science, survey data, and machine
      learning. Her research includes the ZTF Bright Transient Survey, metamorphic supernovae, and AI-enabled
      classification tools such as CCSNscore.

   .. grid-item-card:: Roger Smith
      :class-card: zs-contact-card

      **Detector Lead**

      Roger Smith is Electronics Group Lead at Caltech's Optical Observatory group and a long-time detector
      and instrumentation engineer. He has led major detector development efforts including the ZTF camera and
      continues to work on advanced focal-plane and low-noise detector systems.
